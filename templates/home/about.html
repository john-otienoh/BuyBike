Our Story

BikeShop started in a small garage with one simple goal: give every cyclist access to premium gear at fair prices. Today we serve over 200,000 riders across 50+ countries.

We partner directly with the world's top manufacturers — Trek, Giant, Specialized, Shimano, and more — to bring you authentic products backed by full manufacturer warranties.
